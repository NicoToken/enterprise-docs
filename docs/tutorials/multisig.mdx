# Create a Multisig wallet

## Prerequisites

Before you can make a multisig wallet, make sure you have the completed following:
- Download Google Chrome
- Install [the Terra Station Chrome Extension](https://docs.terra.money/learn/terra-station/Download/terra-station-extension)
- At least two [Terra wallet addresses](https://docs.terra.money/learn/terra-station/Download/terra-station-extension#create-a-wallet)

## Connect your wallet to Enterprise

1. Sign in to your Terra Station wallet using the Terra Station Browser Extension. 

2. To connect your wallet to Enterprise, click the wallet icon in the upper right corner of the screen. 

3. Select **Terra Station Wallet**. 

## Create a new multisig wallet

1. Click on the plus sign on the left side of the screen and select **Multisig Wallet**. 

2. Input a name for your wallet and upload a logo.Click **Next**.

3. Add the wallet addresses of wallets you want to be part of the multisig account and add their weights. Click **Next**.

4. Define your threshold, quorum, unlocking period, and vote duration parameters. Click **Next**.

5. Enter any social media links you wish to add. Click **Next**.

6. Input the password for your Terra Station browser extension wallet and click **Post**.

Congratulations! you just made a multisig wallet!
